🔹 INTRODUCTION TO MYSELF

Hi, I am Rubel Rajbanshi, currently pursuing Masters on Geoinformatics from CSRE Department.My academic path has been a blend of core geology and modern digital tools — from interpreting satellite imagery to deploying web-based GIS platforms.
Since my first day at IITB, I’ve gradually familiarized myself with the campus culture, academic rigor, and diverse opportunities that this institute offers. The journey wasn’t just about labs and lectures — it was also about adapting to the IITB lifestyle, navigating through new challenges, and forming meaningful friendships along the way.
Hostel life has been a big part of this experience — late-night discussions, chai at the hostel gate, random group studies, and the comforting chaos that makes IITB feel like a second home. These moments helped me grow, not just academically, but also personally.
Looking ahead, I aim to use my combined understanding of geology and geospatial tech to contribute to fields like mineral exploration, disaster management, and environmental monitoring.
